# Galaxy Metagenomics Workflow
# Copy-paste commands for Galaxy web interface

## Step 1: Upload Data
- Click "Upload Data"
- Choose FASTQ files
- Format: fastqsanger.gz
- Click "Start"

## Step 2: Quality Control
Tool: FastQC
- Input: Your FASTQ files
- Execute

Tool: Trim Galore!
- Reads 1: R1.fastq
- Reads 2: R2.fastq
- Execute

## Step 3: Assembly
Tool: MEGAHIT
- Forward reads: trimmed_R1
- Reverse reads: trimmed_R2
- Minimum contig length: 1500
- Execute

## Step 4: Binning
Tool: MetaBAT2
- Assembly: contigs.fasta
- BAM files: mapped_reads.bam
- Minimum contig length: 2500
- Execute

## Step 5: Quality Check
Tool: CheckM lineage_wf
- Bins folder: MetaBAT2 bins
- Execute

## Step 6: Taxonomy
Tool: GTDB-Tk classify
- Bins: CheckM filtered bins
- Execute

## Step 7: Download Results
- Click download icon
- Save bins, taxonomy, stats
